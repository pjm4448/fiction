### **1. Foreshadowing (Day 1)** - ~50-75 lines
**Location:** After thrall pen scene (around line 285)
**Content:** 
```
### The Warning

In the darkness, an older voice spoke—barely a whisper. Someone Elandor couldn't see in the crush of bodies.

"You're the paladin."

Not a question. An observation.

Elandor said nothing. Admission was dangerous.

"I knew one once," the voice continued. Raspy. Exhausted. "In another city. Another festival. Twenty years ago."

Pause. Breathing in the darkness.

"He was like you. Disciplined. Faithful. Accumulated power through prayer and righteousness. Kept it contained, compressed, ready for the moment he'd need it."

The voice shifted, coming slightly closer.

"You know what happened?"

Elandor waited.

"The moment came. He released everything he'd stored. Every day of discipline, every prayer, every act of resistance—all at once." A bitter laugh. "He exploded. Not metaphorically. His power had nowhere to go. Feedback loop. He imploded into ash while trying to save us."

Silence.

"The forge burned too hot," the voice whispered. "That's what the old teachings say. 'Discipline is a blade—too sharp, and it cuts the hand that wields it.'"

"Then what should I do?" Elandor asked quietly. "Not resist?"

"I don't know. I just... I saw your tattoo. Saw how you hold yourself. And I thought you should know." The voice was fading, pulling away. "Maybe you'll find a way he didn't. Maybe not. But someone should warn you."

"Who was he?" Elandor asked. "The paladin?"

"My son," the voice said. And said nothing more.
```
